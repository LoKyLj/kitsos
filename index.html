About us

WE BELIEVE IN QUALITY

SINCE 1990

Kitsos Constructions has been serving the market for over 30 years. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing high-quality construction services to our clients. We specialize in both residential and commercial projects, and we take pride in our ability to deliver outstanding results on time and within budget.

Our success is built on our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and attention to detail. We understand that every project is unique, and we work closely with our clients to ensure that their vision is brought to life.

COMMUNICATION IS KEY

We keep our clients informed throughout the entire construction process, from the initial consultation to the final walk-through. We are always available to answer any questions or concerns our clients may have, and we strive to exceed their expectations in every way.

We are proud of our reputation as a trusted and reliable construction company, and we look forward to continuing to serve our clients for many years to come.
